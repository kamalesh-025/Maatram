Welcome to Karpom Karpippom

Karpom Karpippom, an initiative by the Maatram Foundation, is dedicated to empowering underprivileged and village students by providing them with access to quality educational resources and mentorship. Our program aims to bridge the educational gap, offering support through tutoring, skill development, and scholarship opportunities. By fostering a nurturing learning environment, we strive to inspire these students to achieve their dreams and break the cycle of poverty, paving the way for a brighter future.
